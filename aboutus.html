Meet Peace - Our Visionary Founder

Born and raised in Carletonville, Peace Mokoena established Amigos & Projects in 2015 with a simple mission: to provide fair and accessible financial solutions to our community. With over 15 years experience in microfinance, Peace has built a company that combines financial expertise with genuine care for our clients.

"We don't just provide loans - we invest in people's dreams and our community's future."
