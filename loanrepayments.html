Step-by-Step Repayment Instructions

  1. Log in to your banking app or visit your bank's website
  2. Select "Payments" or "Send Money" option
  3. Enter our bank details as shown above
  4. Input the amount you wish to repay
  5. Use your Loan ID or Full Name as the payment reference
  6. Review the details carefully before submitting
  7. Keep the payment confirmation for your records

Payment Processing Time

Payments may take 1-3 business days to reflect in our system. Your account will be updated as soon as we receive confirmation from our bank.
